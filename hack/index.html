About HackRx 6.0

HackRx 6.0 is Bajaj Finserv's flagship annual hackathon exclusively for engineering students across India, focusing on cutting-edge applications of Large Language Models (LLMs) in the financial sector.

This year's edition challenges participants to develop innovative solutions using AI and LLMs to solve real-world problems in banking, insurance, and financial services.

Eligibility

Who Can Participate?

  • B.E./B.Tech students (2026, 2027, 2028 batches)
  • Teams of 1-4 members from same college

Not Eligible?

  • Non-engineering students
  • Graduates or postgraduates
  • Professionals

Skills Needed

  • Programming (Python preferred)
  • Basic AI/ML knowledge
  • Interest in LLMs and fintech

Problem Themes

AI-Powered Chatbots

Build intelligent chatbots for customer service in banking and insurance.

Document Processing

Automate extraction and analysis of financial documents using LLMs.

Fraud Detection

Develop AI models to detect financial fraud and suspicious activities.

Personalized Finance

Create solutions for personalized financial advice using LLMs.
